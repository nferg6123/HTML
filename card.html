Puppies!
(or Dogs)

There have been some studies that suggest owning a dog can help you live longer!

  • Habitat: Everywhere, though some breeds, such as the husky, prefer a colder environment based on their coat. Others might prefer a warmer climate!
  • Lifespan: Varies based on breed. Larger breeds generally have a shorter lifespan, whereas small dogs have a longer one!
  • Weight: Varies based on breeds. There have been some dogs known to get up to over 200 pounds!
  • Adorable Level: Unquantifiable!

Dogs are known as man's best friend. This is for a reason. They are lovable, cuddable, goofy and just fun to be around. They come in all shapes and sizes. They may take work to keep trained, fed and looked after, however the rewards are unlimited. The love and kindness received back from a dog is unlike any other.
